## Frameworks

### Sequoia's 10-slide structure

A 10-slide structure published by Sequoia Capital.

1. Company purpose — one sentence.
2. Problem.
3. Solution.
4. Why now — what's changed in the world.
5. Market size — TAM/SAM/SOM.
6. Competition.
7. Product — screenshots, demo.
8. Business model — pricing, unit economics.
9. Team.
10. Financials — historical and projected.

### Y Combinator pitch structure

A pitch structure used by YC partners in office hours.

1. What does your company do?
2. What's the size of the market?
3. What's your unique insight?
4. How are you doing? (traction)
5. What's your unique advantage?
6. What's your business model?
7. Who's the team?
8. How much are you raising?

### Mike Maples' minimum viable pitch

A pitch structure published by Mike Maples (Floodgate).

- Mission.
- Insight.
- Magic (the demo).
- Method.
- Math.
- Mafia (team).
- Money (the ask).

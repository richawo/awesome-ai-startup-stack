## Free GPU notebooks

- [Modal](https://modal.com) — Python-first serverless GPU runtime. Includes $30/month free compute credit.
- [Kaggle Notebooks](https://www.kaggle.com/code) — Free P100 or T4 GPU access (~30 hours/week) and TPU v3-8 access (~20 hours/week).
- [Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com) — Free T4 GPU access in Jupyter notebooks. Paid tiers (Colab Pro/Pro+) extend session limits.
- [Lightning AI Studios](https://lightning.ai) — Free tier with monthly GPU credits.
- [Hugging Face Spaces](https://huggingface.co/spaces) — Free CPU-backed Spaces. ZeroGPU Spaces (shared A100) available with HF Pro ($9/month).
- [Saturn Cloud](https://saturncloud.io) — Free Jupyter tier with monthly hours.
- [Deepnote](https://deepnote.com) — Free CPU tier.
- [Paperspace Gradient (DigitalOcean)](https://www.paperspace.com/gradient) — GPU notebooks.

## Free serverless compute

- [Modal](https://modal.com) — $30/month free compute credit.
- [Fly.io](https://fly.io) — Free allotment of small VMs.
- [Cloudflare Workers](https://workers.cloudflare.com) — 100k requests/day on the free tier.
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com) — Free Hobby tier with serverless functions and edge middleware.
- [Render](https://render.com) — Free tier for web services (sleeps on inactivity).
- [Railway](https://railway.app) — $5/month trial credit.
- [Deno Deploy](https://deno.com/deploy) — Free tier for edge functions.
- [Replit](https://replit.com) — Free dev environment.
- [GitHub Codespaces](https://github.com/features/codespaces) — 60 hours/month free for personal accounts.
- [Glitch](https://glitch.com) — Free tier for small Node apps.

## Free LLM API credits

- [Groq](https://groq.com) — Free API tier (rate-limited). Llama, Mixtral, Whisper.

- [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com) — Free Gemini API access (rate-limited per minute and day).
- [Together AI](https://www.together.ai) — Signup credit and persistently-free open models.
- [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai) — Free open-model access (rate-limited).
- [Hugging Face Inference API](https://huggingface.co/docs/api-inference) — Free serverless inference for thousands of open models, rate-limited.
- [Mistral Le Plateforme](https://console.mistral.ai) — Free tier.
- [Cohere](https://cohere.com) — Trial keys with limits.
- [Anthropic Console](https://console.anthropic.com) — Initial signup credit.
- [OpenAI](https://platform.openai.com) — Initial signup credit.
- [Replicate](https://replicate.com) — Initial free credit on signup.

## Free databases

- [Supabase](https://supabase.com) — Free tier: 500MB Postgres, 1GB file storage, 2GB bandwidth, Auth, real-time.
- [Neon](https://neon.tech) — Free Postgres with branching, 0.5GB storage.
- [Turso](https://turso.tech) — Free tier: 9GB storage, 1B row reads/month.
- [Convex](https://www.convex.dev) — Free tier with reactive queries.
- [MongoDB Atlas M0](https://www.mongodb.com/atlas) — Free 512MB cluster.
- [Firebase (Spark plan)](https://firebase.google.com/pricing) — Free Firestore, Auth, Functions tier.
- [Cloudflare D1](https://developers.cloudflare.com/d1/) — Free tier: 5GB storage, 5M reads/day.
- [Upstash](https://upstash.com) — Free serverless Redis, Kafka, Vector tiers.
- [Xata](https://xata.io) — Free tier with full-text search.
- [Pinecone](https://pinecone.io) — Free Starter tier for vector DB.
- [Qdrant Cloud](https://cloud.qdrant.io) — 1GB free vector DB.
- [Chroma Cloud](https://www.trychroma.com) — Free tier for hosted Chroma.

## Advertising

- [Google Ads](https://ads.google.com) — Search and display, the largest paid channel for B2B and search-intent products.
- [Meta Ads](https://www.facebook.com/business/ads) — Facebook + Instagram, the largest social-driven paid channel.
- [TikTok Ads](https://ads.tiktok.com) — Short-form video paid, increasingly serious for B2B too.
- [LinkedIn Ads](https://business.linkedin.com/marketing-solutions/ads) — Expensive but unmatched B2B targeting.
- [X Ads](https://ads.x.com) — Twitter/X advertising.
- [Reddit Ads](https://ads.reddit.com) — Underrated for niche communities and dev/AI audiences.
- [Microsoft Ads](https://ads.microsoft.com) — Bing + LinkedIn audience.
- [Snap Ads](https://forbusiness.snapchat.com) — Younger demos.
- [Pinterest Ads](https://ads.pinterest.com) — High-intent, especially for visual products.
- [AdRoll](https://www.adroll.com) — Retargeting platform.
- [Mutiny](https://www.mutinyhq.com) — Personalize your landing pages by visitor segment.

## SEO

- [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com) — SEO research and competitive analysis suite.
- [SEMrush](https://www.semrush.com) — Ahrefs alternative, broader marketing platform.
- [Moz](https://moz.com) — The original SEO toolkit.
- [Surfer SEO](https://surferseo.com) — Content optimization with NLP.
- [Clearscope](https://www.clearscope.io) — Content optimization, competitor against Surfer.
- [MarketMuse](https://www.marketmuse.com) — AI-driven content strategy.
- [Frase](https://www.frase.io) — AI content brief generator.
- [Screaming Frog](https://www.screamingfrog.co.uk/seo-spider/) — Desktop site crawler. Indispensable.
- [AnswerThePublic](https://answerthepublic.com) — Visualizes "people also ask" queries.
- [Ubersuggest](https://neilpatel.com/ubersuggest/) — Cheaper Ahrefs alternative.
- [Keysearch](https://www.keysearch.co) — Affordable keyword research.
- [Lowfruits](https://lowfruits.io) — Find low-competition long-tail keywords.
- [Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console) — Free and essential.
- [Sistrix](https://www.sistrix.com) — Strong in EU markets.
- [Pagespeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev) — Free Core Web Vitals checks.
- [Schema App](https://www.schemaapp.com) — Schema markup tool.

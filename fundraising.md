## Reading List for Founders

See [resources/founder-reading-list.md](resources/founder-reading-list.md) for the full version. Highlights:

- Paul Graham's essays — paulgraham.com
- The Hard Thing About Hard Things — Ben Horowitz
- Zero to One — Peter Thiel

- Founders at Work — Jessica Livingston
- High Output Management — Andy Grove
- Venture Deals — Brad Feld + Jason Mendelson (must-read for fundraising)
- Secrets of Sand Hill Road — Scott Kupor (Andreessen Horowitz)
